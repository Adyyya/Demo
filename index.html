About G P Miraj

Government Polytechnic, Miraj is a premier institution of technical education and research in Maharashtra. Established in 1990, it provides quality education in Civil, Computer, Mechanical, Medical Electronics, and Polymer Technology.

All the programmes are recognised by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to MSBTE, Mumbai.

The institute is situated near MIDC area which strengthens the Industry-Institute relationship. Institute is committed to developing technologically competent industry-ready technicians having high values and national concern. The institute has won the General Championship Trophy for its overall performance in DIPEX-2018.
