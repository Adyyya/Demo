Vision and Mission

Vision: To produce competent computer engineering technicians of high moral values and ethics to serve the community and the continuously changing global industrial needs.

Mission:

  1. To empower the students with the required skills to solve the broad based problems of modern society.
  2. To impart training with an emphasis on promoting collaborative and multidisciplinary activities.
  3. To develop adaptability for working in teams and as individuals in competitive environments with effective communication.
  4. To inculcate the ability for environment-friendly solutions using ethical engineering practices.
